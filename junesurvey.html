General reminders/announcements

I asked those that were recently promoted about how they found the information in the promotion guide. Some people said that it was good and others said that there were things missing. I have told HRs about what they think was missing in the guides so that should hopefully be fixed!

MRs are now not allowed to join servers 40 minutes before, it has been changed to 30 minutes after getting a suggestion and having the majority of HRs agree.

Someone complained about how things are kept confidential, it’s done for different reasons but if you knew everything rank wise then I feel like it wouldn’t be as exciting getting promoted.
